About Me

Hello, I'm James and welcome to my portfolio page! I've always had an interest in technology so I found my passion and desire to create websites. The reason I wanted to get into Web Development was because of my mother and her inability to navigate webpages. She never really got onboard with the internet when she was younger so she is slow at processing what she see's on the page. With this in mind I want to create simplistic but multifaceted web pages and applications with a high focus on UI and UX making the pages easy to navigate no matter how experienced the user is with webpages and applications.
